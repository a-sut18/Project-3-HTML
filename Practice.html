Practice Makes Perfect

Like All Things, Golf is no Different. To Get Better, Practice is a Must

In order to get better at golf and reach my goal of scratch, I must practice nearly daily. Being born good at golf is possible, but being consistent with practice will get you to lengths that are unimaginable. My plan is to hit golf balls at the range on Sunday, Monday, Wednesday, Friday and Saturday. This will help me hone in on my swing and get in work that will prove useful when it is time to execute on the course.
