Staying in Shape and Exercising is Key

No, Golf Isn't the Most Physcially Demanding Sport. Yes, to be the 1% You Must be Healthy

While you don't need to be able to run a marathon to compete in a high level in golf, being in shape matters. More specifically, building arm and leg muscles, stretching and continuing cardio to create a flexible healthy body and swing is a must. I plan on working out Mon - Thursday and then Sundays at my aparment gym complex in order to stay in shape and work on creating a good build.
